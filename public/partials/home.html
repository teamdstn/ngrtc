Welcome to GitTogether

GitTogether is powered by WebRTC and the vLine real-time cloud.

Getting Started

  • Your contacts are the people you you follow on GitHub and the members of your GitHub organizations.
  • To find other GitHub users, click People Search.
  • Don't see anyone you know online? Open another browser window and call yourself (be sure to turn down the volume).
  • Using an external mic and not getting any audio? Star this issue and restart Chrome.
